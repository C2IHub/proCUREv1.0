4. Risk Management

Predictive Risk Analysis
Multi-layered risk mitigation with escalation workflows and predictive analytics

Risk Assessment Framework

Comprehensive risk evaluation across five critical dimensions with weighted scoring and predictive modeling to identify potential issues before they impact operations.

Financial Risk (25%)

Credit ratings, cash flow analysis, debt-to-equity ratios, and financial stability indicators.

Operational Risk (25%)

Production capacity, facility redundancy, operational procedures, and business continuity planning.

Quality Trend Risk (20%)

Quality metrics trends, audit results, customer complaints, and corrective action effectiveness.

Supply Chain Risk (15%)

Supplier dependencies, geographic concentration, logistics vulnerabilities, and alternative sourcing options.

Regulatory Risk (15%)

Compliance history, regulatory changes impact, inspection results, and regulatory relationship quality.

Mitigation Strategies

Emergency Supplier Activation

Immediate activation of pre-qualified backup suppliers with 3-5 day implementation timeline. High cost but 90% effectiveness for critical supply continuity.

Expedited Certification Review

Fast-track certification renewal processes with regulatory body coordination. 7-10 day timeline with medium cost and 75% effectiveness rate.

Inventory Buffer Increase

Temporary safety stock level increases to mitigate short-term supply risks. 1-2 day implementation with low cost and 60% effectiveness.
